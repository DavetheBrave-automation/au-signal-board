Bot Fleet — The Orchestra

"Don't just listen to the drum — let the whole orchestra play the symphony."
Each bot reads ONE dimension of the market. The signal is in the HARMONY between all five voices.

Layer 1 — Eagle (Phase Detector)

What it reads
Retail sentiment via CVD (Cumulative Volume Delta) and buy/sell pressure cycles
Output
CAPITULATION · ACCUMULATION · NEUTRAL · BREAKOUT_ATTEMPT + CVD direction
SignalMeaningAction
CAPITULATIONRetail panic selling, sellers exhaustingWatch for Nano disagreement (bullish if Nano stays ACCUM)
ACCUMULATIONBuying pressure buildingMomentum aligning upward
NEUTRALNo strong directional pressureWait for catalyst
BREAKOUT_ATTEMPTStrong buying surge detectedBreakout in progress — act fast
Key rule: Eagle is the LOUDEST signal but NOT the most important. It tells you what RETAIL is doing — which is often wrong at turns.

Layer 2 — Nano (Perp Structure Analyzer)

What it reads
Perpetual futures OI, funding rates, institutional positioning across 9 perp-equipped assets
Output
ACCUMULATION · DISTRIBUTION · CAPITULATION + funding rate + OI change
THE critical signal: Nano ACCUM during Eagle CAP = institutions buying what retail is selling = the STRONGEST bullish signal in the system. This is the foundation of the Symphony Protocol.
PatternRead
Eagle CAP + Nano ACCUMBottom forming — institutions loading during panic
Eagle CAP + Nano CAPREAL breakdown — even institutions leaving
7+/9 Nano ACCUMSYMPHONY OVERRIDE — fleet-wide institutional consensus
Nano DISTRIBUTION on individual assetInstitutions taking profit — exit or don't enter

Layer 3 — Osprey (Order Flow Monitor)

What it reads
Whale transactions, zone positioning (0-100%), volume spikes, PIRATE/FENCE events
Output
Zone position · Volume ratio · PIRATE signals · FENCE ACTIVE · Zone accumulation patterns
Filtering rule: Osprey fires HUNDREDS of signals per session. Must be filtered through Eagle + Nano for context. Individual SELL signals at resistance = whale profit-taking, not reversals. PIRATE CLUSTERS across hours = high conviction accumulation.

Layer 4 — Vulture (Technical Indicator Engine)

What it reads
EMA stack (9/21), RSI, R:R ratio across multiple timeframes
Output
BULLISH STACK · BEARISH STACK · FLAT · RSI levels · R:R assessment
Key principle: Vulture is the REFERENCE GUIDE (lagging), not the SIGNAL (leading). The bots are the leading indicators; Vulture confirms they're right. EMA STACK flips are DURABLE — they don't reverse in hours like Eagle/CVD.

Layer 5 — Weatherman (Macro Context Scanner)

What it reads
Fear & Greed Index, on-chain metrics, scheduled macro events, geopolitical context
Output
Weatherman score · F&G index · On-chain score · Event calendar
The concert hall: "Don't just listen to the song — know what concert hall you're in." Same bot signal means completely different things in different macro environments. MANDATORY check before any trade analysis.

Key Trading Rules

80% ROI Minimum — Never buy deep ITM contracts (70¢+). Buy near the strike when price is AT the floor/ceiling.

Capital Rotation — Don't hold to settlement unless forced. Buy at 50¢, sell at 75-80¢, redeploy. Active rotation beats passive holding.

SMART MONEY EXIT — When bots detect institutional profit-taking at resistance while position is green, EXIT IMMEDIATELY. Do NOT wait for TP targets.

20% Cash Reserve — Always keep 20% for midweek opportunities. Never deploy more than 80%.

SOL Excluded — 0-1 record, inverted funding rate, weakest signal. Not tradeable until proven otherwise.
